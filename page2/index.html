    Research Interests

    My current research focuses on advancing privacy-preserving technologies for machine learning and secure data analytics. I explore cutting-edge methods such as federated learning, homomorphic encryption, differential privacy, and secure multiparty computation to enhance data security while enabling robust and efficient computations. My ultimate goal is to design innovative solutions that achieve an optimal balance between privacy, scalability, and performance in real-world applications.
